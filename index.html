Pest Coverage
What's Included & What's Not

✓ Included at Flat Rate

  • Cockroaches, all species including German roach
  • Palmetto bugs and American roaches
  • General ants (exclusions noted)
  • Spiders
  • Silverfish and firebrats
  • Rodents — rats and mice, interior and exterior
  • House flies and drain flies
  • Stored product pests
  • Occasional invaders: crickets, earwigs, centipedes
  • General household and commercial pest insects

✗ Not Covered — Quoted Separately

  • Bed bugs — quoted and billed per occurrence
  • Fire ants, Pharaoh ants, Carpenter ants
  • Fleas and ticks
  • Bees, wasps, and hornets
  • Wildlife, snakes, and nuisance animals
  • Termites and wood-destroying organisms
  • Stinging or venomous insects

Non-covered pest services are always quoted prior to service. Emergency response for non-covered pests: $180.00 per visit.
